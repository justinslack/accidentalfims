The Adventures of Team Kazi in the Eastern Cape

In 2007, as a partner in Free Range Films, I produced Borderlines, a documentary directed by Khayakazi Soldati and commissioned by SABC 1. Filming of Borderlines took us from Cape Town along the southern coast of South Africa to Mthatha. The team on the road was myself, Khayakazi Soldati, Karen Landsberg (camera) and Ndumiso Ngcobo (sound).

The film followed Khaya’s journey to understand her family’s political past in the Transkei during the turbulent days of apartheid.
